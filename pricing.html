Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some countries, bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for students?

We offer a 20% discount for students with a valid .edu email address or student ID. Contact our support team to verify your student status and receive your discount code.

Is there a family or group plan?

We offer special pricing for groups of 3 or more people. Please contact our sales team for more information about group plans and enterprise solutions.

What's included in the free trial?

Our 7-day free trial gives you full access to all courses and features, equivalent to our Premium plan. You can explore all content and cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.
